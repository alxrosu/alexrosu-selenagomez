I Said I Love You First

Album Details

I Said I Love You First not only is the couple’s first joint album but is also Selena’s return to music after hinting at stepping away from music in December 2024 to focus on acting.

In a playful Instagram post announcing the album, she teased fans: “I always trick you guys.” Blanco also announced the album on Instagram, later sharing a heartfelt post featuring behind-the-scenes video clips from the couple’s album photoshoot: “I can’t believe I get to spend the rest of my life with you,” he wrote.

On February 13, 2025, “Scared of Loving You” was released as the album’s first promotional single. On February 20, 2025, “Call Me When You Break Up” featuring Gracie Abrams was released as the official lead single.
